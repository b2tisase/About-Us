THE BITEW FOUNDATION (TBF)
The purpose of the foundation is: Basic education, social activism and development in Ethiopia
Social and economic development in Ethiopia can only be achieved when we educate children who have not had access to education!

Learn more and start today

What is TBF?

TBF is a volunteer group composed of young adults from the United States who are concerned about underprivileged children in Ethiopia. Our mission is to secure a progressive future for Ethiopian children by improving their access to education so they can gain basic literacy and vocational skills.

What do we do?

Our work supports volunteer agencies in Ethiopia that are directly involved with the development of educational and other programs for underprivileged children. In the United States, we raise money through various fundraisers and cultural events.

What is a project?

A TBF project can be defined as providing:

  • Financial support for educational and development activities in a community.
  • Volunteers working closely with the community and NGO.
  • Collaboration with other NGOs.
  • TBF strives to identify other non-governmental organizations with similar goals.

We do not restrict ourselves to literacy-based education initiatives. We consider all forms of education that may act as powerful agents of social change and empowerment.
